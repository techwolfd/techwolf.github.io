My metal family

Metal Family is an ongoing Russian web series created in 2018 by Alina Kovaleva.
It goes into the family of Victorya Winslow and Sebastian "Glam" Shvagenbagen along
with their two children Dee and Heavy all of whom adore metal music and other people avoid due
to their unconventional lifestyle.

About

The title card is done by the family's key-holder,
which consists on the words "Metal Family" made of literal metal with four nails as hooks.
The family members who are in the episode will already have put their keys on their hooks,
with the focus character just hanging theirs up and rock music can be heard in the background.
